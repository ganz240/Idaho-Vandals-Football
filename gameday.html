The Kibbie Dome

The Kibbie Dome in Moscow, Idaho is where the University of Idaho Vandals Football Team plays their home games. Built in 1974 with a capacity of 15,250 it was the smallest football stadium in FBS when the Vandals competed in that subdivison. This small enclosed football venue has a cult following thanks to the EA Sports NCAA Football 2014 video game and reputation of loud rowdy fans that can fill it with noise if the "Dome gets a Rockin." Games usually kickoff at 2pm Pacific time and tailgating at the adjacent parking lot to the West begins at 9am.
