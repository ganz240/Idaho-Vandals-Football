Early Years

The University of Idaho Started its football program in 1893, where they played other college teams, athlletic football clubs, and high school teams throughout the Americian Northwest. In 1921 Idaho joined the Pacific Coast Conference (PCC), which increased the competion and was in the PCC until 1958 when both Idaho and Montana where left behind as the other schools formed the PAC-10.

Later Years

Following the dissolution of the PCC, the Vandals went on to bounce around conferences from the Big Sky, to the Big West, to the WAC, to the Sun Belt, and back to the Big Sky, where they currently compete. The Vandals had some successful seasons winning 11 conference championships and 3 Bowl victories. Currently the last 3 years under Coach Eck have arguably been the best three seasons in a row going 26 Wins and 13 Losess with 3 consecutive seasons of FCS Playoff appearances.
